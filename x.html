The Problem

  • Customers are fragmented across banks + fintechs; we risk losing touch as they adopt multi-bank dashboards.
  • Competitors and fintechs are integrating deeply; ENBD must defend against disintermediation.
  • Legacy systems inhibit rapid innovation and API monetization; we lack a unified, scalable platform.

Our Vision: ENBD Open Finance Platform

  • Secure, consent-driven APIs for account aggregation, payments, data insights, embedded services.
  • Platform for fintechs, corporate clients, and internal ENBD teams to build on.
  • One architecture to enable cross-sell, faster innovation, and third-party monetization.

Use Cases & Stakeholder Value

  • Consumer: Personal finance dashboards, unified view, smarter offers via data insights.
  • SME / Corporate: Cashflow APIs, instant payments, embedded lending or treasury services.
  • Fintech / Partners: Access to ENBD’s data / APIs, revenue share model, co-branded products.

Competitive Benchmarking

  • Global banks (UK, Europe) have unlocked multi-million USD API revenue streams. [oai_citation:5‡PwC](https://www.strategyand.pwc.com/m1/en/strategic-foresight/sector-strategies/financial-services/open-banking/open-banking.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
  • Regional peers in KSA & UAE exploring open & embedded finance. [oai_citation:6‡thepaypers.com](https://thepaypers.com/fintech/expert-views/open-banking-in-the-gcc-picking-up-pace?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
  • ENBD’s advantage: scale, trust, existing API experience, brand & distribution.

Business & Monetization Model

  • API usage / subscription fees charged to fintechs, platform users.
  • Embedded finance revenue share (loans, payments, insurance).
  • Cross-sell uplift: +5% increase in product attachment via data insights.
  • Cost savings: 20–30% lower acquisition and servicing via API orchestration.

Roadmap: Pilot → Scale

  • Pilot (Q4 2025 – Q2 2026): 2 core APIs (aggregation + payments), 5 fintech partners, internal validation.
  • Phase 2 (Q3 2026 – Q4 2027): Expand APIs, onboard 20–30 partners, start monetization.
  • Scale (2028+): Full open finance ecosystem, regional expansion, new verticals (wealth, insurance).

Key Risks & Mitigations

  • Regulatory Delay: Close engagement with UAE Central Bank & GCC regulators; incremental compliance gating.
  • Data Security / Privacy: ISO 27001, strong encryption, consent frameworks, third-party audits.
  • Adoption Risk: Co-creation with fintechs, incentive models, pilot success metrics, phased rollout.

Financials & Return on Investment

  • Total Investment: USD 30 million over 3 years (Capex + Opex).
  • Revenue Projection (Base Case): USD 8 million in Year 2, USD 25 million in Year 3.
  • Cost Savings & Uplift: ~USD 5 million equivalent via lower CAC, improved retention.
  • Payback: Expected in Month 30 (~2.5 years).
  • Upside Scenario: If adoption is strong, revenue could exceed USD 40 million by Year 3.
