ENBD Open Finance: CEO Decision Brief

Market Leadership, Revenue Growth & Strategic Value
  • Market Window: MEA open banking market to USD 10B by 2030, UAE regulatory readiness, 3–5 year first-mover window
  • ENBD Advantage: 33% banking share, largest UAE API network, 97% digital adoption
  • Value Creation: USD 2.7BN new revenue, USD 7.2BN cumulative through 2030, 78% revenue CAGR in open finance
  • Strategic Alignment: Direct support for Vision 2030: digital leadership, SME focus, new business models
DECISION SNAPSHOT
Approving this investment cements ENBD as UAE & GCC open finance leader, drives substantial revenue/ROI, and strengthens national innovation agenda.
Emirates NBD | CEO Investment Presentation | October 2025

Investment Ask & Value Delivery

  • Requested Investment: USD 180M across 5 years (2025–2030)
  • Allocation:
    • Platform & tech delivery: 50%
    • Partner & ecosystem onboarding: 25%
    • Regulatory/compliance: 15%
    • Innovation pilots: 10%
  • Financial Impact: Breakeven in 2 years, USD 7.2Bn cumulative revenue, 15–18% of ENBD total by 2030
STRATEGIC FIT
This investment transforms ENBD into a core digital infrastructure provider for the UAE/GCC—and delivers rapid, scalable financial results.
Approval will activate dedicated cross-functional teams and board-level performance oversight.
