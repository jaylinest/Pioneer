STRATEGIC RESPONSE
Five Pillars of Competitive Defense
1
Launch Digital FX Platform
Create "ENBD FX Direct" - digital-only platform with 0.8-1.2% spreads, instant transfers, transparent pricing. White-label fintech infrastructure to compete on speed and UX.
2
Ecosystem Lock-In Strategy
Bundle FX with high-margin products (credit cards, mortgages, wealth management). Offer preferential FX rates tied to total relationship value. Increase switching costs.
3
Corporate & SME Fortification
Defend B2B segment with integrated trade finance, treasury services, multi-currency accounts, and API banking. Services Wise cannot replicate.
4
Strategic Partnerships
Partner with remittance corridors (India, Pakistan, Philippines) for competitive pricing. Consider minority investment in complementary fintechs. Explore Wise partnership for specific segments.
5
Margin Diversification & Value Migration
Accept 30-40% FX margin compression but compensate through: (a) Increased lending to FX customers at higher margins, (b) Cross-sell wealth management products, (c) Transaction banking fees, (d) Premium service tiers, (e) Treasury and hedging products for corporates. Shift from transactional to relationship profitability model.
